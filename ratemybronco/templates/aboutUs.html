What is Rate My Bronco?


Our web service will act as a tool for CPP Computer Science students to get ratings of courses, professors, and view grade distributions all tailored to courses specifically taught here at CPP. We hope to provide meaning statistics and visuals that will help students make educated decisions when choosing their next courses.
